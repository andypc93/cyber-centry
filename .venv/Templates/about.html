What is CyberSentry?

Advanced Intrusion Detection

CyberSentry is an advanced Intrusion Detection System (IDS) empowered by machine learning capabilities. Our innovative solution effectively classifies and identifies malware, enhancing your digital security measures. Utilizing a Flask-based executable, CyberSentry operates seamlessly within your web environment, providing robust protection against cyber threats with unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.
